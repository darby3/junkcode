LEGO® Cinema

LEGO® Oberlin’s historic movie theater welcomes minifigs of all ages via the brick-built entrance and outdoor ticket booth. The theatre’s gold and red brick marquee flashes the title of each move. One of few remaining single-screen theaters in the country, LEGO® Apollo shows first-run movies at reasonable prices. Inside, get comfy in one the 400+ seats surrounded by the signature zig zag LEGO® design and vinyl padded walls.
